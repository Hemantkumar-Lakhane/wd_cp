About Cultural Department

Dedicated to preserving and promoting India's diverse cultural heritage

The Cultural Department of the Government of India serves as the custodian of our nation's rich and diverse cultural heritage. We are committed to preserving, promoting, and propagating the myriad forms of art, traditions, and cultural practices that define India's identity.

Our mission encompasses supporting artists, organizing cultural events, maintaining heritage sites, and fostering cultural exchange both within India and internationally. We believe that culture is the soul of a nation, and through our various initiatives, we strive to keep India's cultural flame burning bright for future generations.

Government Initiatives

Transforming India's cultural landscape through innovative programs

Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat

Enhancing interaction between people of diverse cultures

Unity in Diversity

This flagship program aims to strengthen the fabric of traditionally existing emotional bonds between people across states and UTs through enhanced interaction.

It promotes mutual learning and appreciation of diverse cultures, traditions, and languages.

Kala Sanskriti Vikas Yojana

Promoting art and cultural education

Cultural Education

A comprehensive scheme for the development of arts and culture education in schools and communities.

Includes training programs, workshops, and infrastructure development for cultural institutions.

International Cultural Exchange

Building bridges across borders through culture

Global Outreach

Facilitating cultural exchanges with countries worldwide to showcase India's rich heritage and learn from global traditions.

Includes artist exchange programs, cultural festivals, and collaborative projects.

Artisan Development Program

Empowering traditional craftspeople

Skill Enhancement

Supporting traditional artisans through skill development, marketing assistance, and financial support.

Creating sustainable livelihoods while preserving ancient crafts and techniques.

Digital Heritage Project

Digitizing India's cultural treasures

Digital Preservation

Creating digital archives of India's cultural heritage for future generations.

Making cultural content accessible through virtual museums and online platforms.

Youth Cultural Program

Engaging the next generation in cultural activities

Future Leaders

Encouraging youth participation in cultural activities through competitions, workshops, and mentorship programs.

Building cultural ambassadors for the future.

Monument Mitra

Adopt a heritage site for development

Heritage Adoption

Corporate entities adopt heritage sites to provide better amenities and enhance the tourism experience.

Ensuring sustainable maintenance of our historical monuments.

Spiritual Circuit

Connecting places of religious significance

Cultural Tourism

Developing infrastructure around major spiritual and religious destinations.

Promoting pilgrimage tourism and cultural understanding.
